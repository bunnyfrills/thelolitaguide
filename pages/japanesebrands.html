Japanese Brands

This list contains all Japanese brands I am aware of and will be updated when necessary. Each brand is represented by a release I believe is iconic and/or displays their current style. Hovering over an image will give information on the brand such as if it is plus size friendly, what style they are usually known for, if they are defunct, etc.
If the page does not load quickly, you can use this text based list.

Angelic Pretty
Mostly known for sweet lolita, but they sometimes release non-sweet and even non-lolita pieces. Has stores in Japan, China, the USA, and France. Not known to be plus size friendly.
Baby the Stars Shine Bright
Mostly known for sweet lolita, but they sometimes release non-sweet and even non-lolita pieces. Has stores in Japan and the USA. Sometimes release plus-size friendly pieces.
Royal Princess Alice
They do everything, mostly gothic. Very busy, detailed prints. Only ships within Japan, does overseas retail with Kuroshiro Kawaii in the US.
